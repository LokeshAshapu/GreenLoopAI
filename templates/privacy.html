Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 06, 2026

1. Information We Collect

GreenLoop AI collects information to enable civilian environmental reporting. This includes:

  • Account Details: Username, email, and password.
  • Contact Information: Phone number and residential address (see Encryption details below).
  • Environmental Reports: Uploaded images, textual hazard descriptions, and GPS coordinates (Latitude & Longitude).

2. Cyber-Security & Field-Level Encryption

We prioritize civilian reporter safety. Sensitive personal data (including phone numbers, physical addresses, and precise hazard descriptions) is processed using AES-256 Fernet encryption prior to database persistence.

Keys are isolated in protected environments. This guarantees that even in the event of a database compromise, all personally identifiable information (PII) remains completely illegible.

3. How We Use Information

Collected data is utilized strictly for platform services:

  • Evaluating uploads using NVIDIA NIM Vision Language Models (VLM).
  • Rendering coordinate pins on interactive Leaflet Maps for public view.
  • Coordinating volunteer cleanup activities and awarding Eco Points.

4. Data Sharing & Third-Party Integrations

We do not sell user data. Images uploaded for environmental scans are securely transmitted to the **NVIDIA NIM API** for automated hazard evaluation. Coordinate points are publicly visible to municipal officers and volunteers to organize cleanups.
